Allow us to introduce ourselves, my name is Erica and my partner is Jolly Joe. We are currently located in Northern California but are more than willing to travel. In between dog walks/cat cuddles, Joe fills his days studying computer programming in order to pursue a career in the technology field and Erica works from home as an Independent Contractor for a virtual contact temp agency.

We're passionate about caring for your furry family members when you can't be there. Our top priority is making sure your dogs and cats feel loved and pampered, with plenty of belly rubs, ear scratches, and snuggles.

Our services include:

  • Providing delicious meals and fresh water
  • Taking dogs on daily walks to explore new sights and smells
  • Keeping litter boxes clean for cats, ensuring a tidy and fresh space
  • Administering any necessary medications with lots of love and cuddles
  • Showering your pets with attention and affection, with plenty of playtime and snuggles
